Prepared FAQs for TYMO BEAUTIES!

November 26, 2021

We Prepared FAQs for TYMO BEAUTIES!  

You have questions, we have answers.

Here are the FAQs we summarized for our beloved customers, and hope uplifting your hairstyling experience.

1. How to use TYMO Styling Products

Straightener Brushes

Straightening Irons 

Hair Curler

  • Blow dry your hair completely, NO wet hair (can use humidity blocker to avoid frizz)
  • Apply TYMO MIST or other heat protectants
  • Detangle your hair completely and section your hair
  • Put TYMO glove on
  • Choose the suitable temperature
  • One small strand for each pass, slowly brush through

Check tutorial from Tea

2. How to use TYMO VOLUMIZER

  • Towel dry your hair prevent from dripping
  • Apply TYMO MIST or other heat protectants
  • Detangle your hair completely and section your hair
  • Choose the suitable temperature and air speed
  • One small strand for each pass, slowly roll in your hair and set for few second

Check tutorial from Julissa

4. What's the difference between TYMO Straighteners?

5. For Type 4?

YES! We take different hair types and textures into consideration to make sure TYMO hair tools are effective for all.

Please check tutorial from Mariam

6. For Short Hair?

YES! TYMO hair tools works perfectly on short hair. Designed in effective shape with deeper and denser teeth that can well hold and define the hair and works perfectly for all hair types and lengths including short hair.

Check tutorial from Mariam

7. For Relaxed Hair?

YES! TYMO hair tools works well for relaxed hair. Chemical lotions or creams applied earlier wont effect the results. We suggest that you apply heat protectant such as TYMO MIST before using. Please note that no flammable hair product should be applied before or during usage.

8. Reach Roots?

TYMO hair tools can get to the roots, the uniquely anti-scalding design and dense heating teeth help reach the hair roots.

Try apply it from top down and from bottom up!

9. On Wet Hair?

Please DO NOT apply on wet hair while using Straightener Brushes, Straightening Irons , Hair Curler or other Styling Products.

10. How to Clean？

Please make sure TYMO styling tools are unplugged and use baby wipes clean barrel or bristles/between teeth gently. Dental floss always helps to reach tricky parts.

*TYMO HYPERSONIC has auto clean function, please refer to manual for instruction.

11. Effects on Curl Pattern?

NO! TYMO Hair tools won’t affect the curl pattern at all, it will go back to the pattern after hair washing.

12. Heat Damage?

TYMO hair products are designed to prevent unnecessary heat damage. We encourage you apply heat protectant such as TYMO MIST before using styling products for healthier finish look and longer lasting style.

Try TYMO VOLUMIST AFTER styling to lock the glamorous!

*TYMO HYPERSONIC is an advanced hair dryer with stable low temperature, it dose not require heat protection.

13. Code Not Working?

Please check BRAND WEBSITE  to check on the most updated CODE and apply the right one for your beloved TYMO product by check out.

14. Does this have a UK/other than US plug?

Most TYMO products are U.S. standard.

We do offer part of the collection in UK plug, please check it on Amazon.

Please purchase an extra adaptor if you are not U.S. based. You can get it in super market or online, the cost of the adaptor is around $10.
