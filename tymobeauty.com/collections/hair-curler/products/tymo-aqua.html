The very first heating tool that HYDRATES your hair!

Sounds hard to believe? TYMO AQUA fulfills the wonder with the most innovative and patented Zeolite Technology. The curler absorbs water from the air and releases millions of aqua ions and anions during heating.

Nano ions can penetrate deeply from the surface into the core of hair, therefore, reduces more than 80% possible heat damage while hydrating hair, resulting healthy and natural shininess.

In short, a Sauna spa treat (expect real steam) towards glowing finish look!

A custom-blend 1.25-inch large titanium barrel (size matters for bouncy curlers) ensures a smoother glide, reducing 50% of breakage comparing to traditional ceramic barrel, meanwhile allows an accurate unique profile based on your hair texture, color and chemical treatments, leaves glamorous finish look that just won’t quit.

About TYMO

TYMO specializes in developing innovative hair styling tools (Hair Straighteners, Flat Irons, Hair Dryers). TYMO exceeds expectation of customers around the world with optimal feedback - TYMO Hair Straightener has topped selling list since it was been first launched two years ago.
